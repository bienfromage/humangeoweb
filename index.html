Human Geography in Websites

One of the major themes of human geography is globalization. Globalization is the exchange of ideas and customs that occurs when cultures interact. Globalization has long been cited as the major source of pop culture in modern society. With the development of the internet, it has become very easy for ideas and customs to diffuse accross long distances in a short amount of time, allowing globalization to occur at an incredible rate. As a result, new standards seep across the world rapidly, until they become the norm from Japan to Texas. An excellent example of this is the modern internet. Thanks to globalization, new styles and conventions for website programming diffuse across the world in a matter of weeks. The result is that many modern websites contain similar elements and styles.
